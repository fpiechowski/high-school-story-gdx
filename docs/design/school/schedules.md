# 📚 School Schedules – *High School Story*

This document defines the weekly class schedules for all three school years. Each day runs from **7:00 AM to ~2:00 PM**, with short breaks between classes and a longer lunch break. The player must attend classes unless scripted otherwise and may choose how to act during class (study, socialize, rest, etc.).

> 📌 Time format: Each period = 45 minutes.  
> Breaks = 15 minutes between classes, 60 minutes for lunch.

## 🧩 Notes

- **Club Hour**: Time used for attending one of the school clubs; social and stat effects apply.
- **Weekly Review**: Reflection/summary slot used for narrative delivery, event triggers, and stress recovery.
- **Mock Exams**: High-pressure class session in Year 3 that influences final exam outcomes.
- **Electives**: Player may influence or customize which elective(s) are taken per semester.
- **Self-Study**: Player may use this period freely (study, relax, socialize).
