# 🧑‍🏫 Classmates Roster – *High School Story*

This document defines the 20 unique classmates who share the player’s homeroom class. Each one is based on a recognizable teen stereotype, with their own academic strengths, interests, and a hidden personal truth that’s revealed only through deep relationship progression.

---

## 📚 Academics & Overachievers

### 🎓 Emily Carter – The Overachiever
- **Knowledge & Skills:** Math, English, Science
- **Preferences:** Color-coded notes, black coffee, classical music
- **Hidden Trait:** Suffers from severe anxiety and breaks down if she ever gets less than an A

---

### 🧠 Noah Brooks – The Quiet Genius
- **Knowledge & Skills:** Physics, Computer Science, Chemistry
- **Preferences:** Stargazing, collecting stones, long walks alone
- **Hidden Trait:** Secretly writes a sci-fi novel inspired by his emotionally absent parents

---

### 🍎 Sarah Kim – The Teacher’s Pet
- **Knowledge & Skills:** History, English, Civics
- **Preferences:** School supplies, oatmeal cookies, volunteering in class
