# ❤️ Relationship Arc – Zoe Reed

## 🎭 Stereotype / Role
> "The Sci-Fi Nerd" – obsessed with Star Wars, space travel, and alternate realities. Zoe lives in fandoms but secretly dreams of living her own heroic story.

---

## 🧠 Base Profile

- **Subjects Proficient In:** Physics, Computer Science, Literature
- **Preferences:** Cosplay, space documentaries, collectible figurines, bubble tea
- **Clubs:** Sci-Fi Club, D&D Campaign Group
- **Schedule Notes:** Found in the library sci-fi section, AV room, or drawing campaign maps

---

## 🔐 Hidden Trait
> Zoe was bullied at her last school and uses fiction to create a world where she always wins, always belongs — and never gets hurt.

---

## 📈 Relationship Progression Stages

### 🧩 Stage 1 – Acquaintance
- **Stat Requirement:** None
- **Trigger:** Player joins a casual lunchtime D&D game
- **Scene Summary:** Zoe plays a rogue hacker elf and instantly critiques everyone's backstory
- **Player Choice Impact:** Compliment her lore = +Rel, roll your eyes = neutral, ignore = –Rel

### 🧩 Stage 2 – Budding Friendship
- **Stat Requirement:** Relationship ≥ 10
- **Trigger:** Help her set up a sci-fi movie night
- **Scene Summary:** Zoe starts rambling about plot holes in a Star Trek episode
- **Player Choice:** Ask more or tease her with trivia
- **Reward:** Access to "movie marathons" for Mood and Stress recovery

### 🧩 Stage 3 – Vulnerability
- **Stat Requirement:** Relationship ≥ 25
- **Trigger:** Notice a scar on her arm during cosplay prep
- **Scene Summary:** She avoids the topic, then later admits she was bullied badly at her last school
- **Player Choice:** Reassure her, open up in return, or simply sit with her
- **Reward:** Unlocks “Holodeck Hangouts” — themed study spots where her comfort stat rises

### 🧩 Stage 4 – Trust
- **Stat Requirement:** Relationship ≥ 50
- **Trigger:** She asks the player to co-write a fanfic or world-build a campaign
- **Scene Summary:** You become the first person she lets shape her story
- **Player Choice:** Create something with her, or support quietly
- **Reward:** Custom item — “Shared World Map,” increasing Mood when kept in dorm

### 🧩 Stage 5 – Emotional Climax
- **Stat Requirement:** Relationship ≥ 75, during Spring Trip
- **Scene Summary:** Zoe gets publicly mocked for her cosplay and freezes
- **Player Choice:** Defend her, distract, or stand by silently
- **Reward:** Heartfelt scene where she thanks the player, calling them her “real crew”

### 🧩 Stage 6 – Relationship Resolution
- **Stat Requirement:** Relationship ≥ 90
- **Trigger:** Final club meeting or Graduation
- **Scene Summary:** Zoe gives you a sealed letter — a story where you're the protagonist
- **Player Choice:** Read with her, hug silently, or give her a story of your own
- **Reward:** Ending shows Zoe becoming a sci-fi author with a dedication: “For my first fellow adventurer.”

---

## 🔁 Optional Recurring Events
- School Comic-Con cosplay contest
- Valentine’s: Hand-made D&D stat sheet about the player
- D&D One-Shot Event with Zoe as DM
