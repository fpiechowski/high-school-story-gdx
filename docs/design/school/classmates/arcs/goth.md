# ❤️ Relationship Arc – Raven Black

## 🎭 Stereotype / Role

> "The Goth" – sarcastic, dressed in black, makes morbid jokes, and keeps others at a distance. Most students find her
> intimidating or weird.

---

## 🧠 Base Profile

- **Subjects Proficient In:** Literature, Art, Psychology
- **Preferences:** Industrial music, tarot cards, black nail polish, horror movies, graveyard walks
- **Clubs:** Art Club (sometimes skips)
- **Schedule Notes:** Often alone during breaks, prefers the library or garden after school

---

## 🔐 Hidden Trait

> Raven volunteers at a local animal shelter on weekends and is obsessed with cuddly animals — especially cats. She's
> terrified of anyone discovering this softer side.

---

## 📈 Relationship Progression Stages

---

### 🧩 Stage 1 – Acquaintance

- **Stat Requirement:** None
- **Trigger:** Random seating assignment or shared Art class
- **Scene Summary:** Raven makes a grim joke about mortality when asked to draw a still life
- **Player Choice Impact:** Laughing earns her curiosity; criticizing earns cold distance
- **Reward:** Raven becomes visible in the art room during club hours

---

### 🧩 Stage 2 – Budding Friendship

- **Stat Requirement:** Relationship ≥ 10
- **Trigger:** Player joins Art Club or chooses to walk home the same way
- **Scene Summary:** Conversation about horror movies and why she prefers fictional fear over real feelings
- **Player Choice:** Ask her favorite movie or make fun of her taste
- **Reward:** +1 Mood after spending time with her, ability to give her sketchbook items

---

### 🧩 Stage 3 – Vulnerability

- **Stat Requirement:** Relationship ≥ 25, Mood ≥ 30
- **Trigger:** Catch her sneaking out early from a club meeting
- **Scene Summary:** She tries to avoid explaining where she’s going — hints she’s “got something important to do”
- **Player Choice:** Offer to walk with her or tease her about being mysterious
- **Reward:** Unlocks the city shelter location (only accessible through her route)

---

### 🧩 Stage 4 – Trust

- **Stat Requirement:** Relationship ≥ 50
- **Trigger:** She invites the player to visit the shelter “only if you promise not to laugh”
- **Scene Summary:** She melts while playing with kittens, confesses it’s the only time she feels safe showing her real
  self
- **Player Choice:** Express admiration or joke to lighten the mood
- **Reward:** Shelter becomes a recurring hangout (boosts Mood), custom gift option unlocked (cat charm, coffee beans)

---

### 🧩 Stage 5 – Emotional Climax

- **Stat Requirement:** Relationship ≥ 75, triggered during Winter Trip
- **Scene Summary:** She opens up during a quiet night walk about feeling fake — her goth persona started as a defense
  mechanism after a traumatic friendship breakup
- **Player Choice:** Encourage her to stay herself or explore other sides
- **Reward:** Unique cutscene unlocked, player can now see her in casual (non-goth) clothes during weekends

---

### 🧩 Stage 6 – Relationship Resolution

- **Stat Requirement:** Relationship ≥ 90
- **Trigger:** During the final Spring Trip or Valentine’s Day event
- **Scene Summary:** Raven confesses that meeting the player helped her reconnect with the side of herself she’d buried
- **Player Choice:** Deep friendship, romance, or respectful parting
- **Reward:** Final photo item (Polaroid with shelter kitten), added to ending credits montage

---

## 🔁 Optional Recurring Events

- **Art Room Sketch Sessions:** Available Tuesdays and Thursdays
- **Halloween Festival Booth:** Runs a tarot stall, opens unique dialogue
- **Christmas Gift Mini-Event:** Only gives you something if you bring a shelter-related gift

---

## 🧪 Design Notes

- Raven’s arc explores the contrast between how someone appears and who they really are
- Offers mood-based bonding moments, a quiet romance route, or deep platonic trust
- Ideal for players who enjoy peeling back emotional layers and helping others heal
