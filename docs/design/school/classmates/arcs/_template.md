## 📈 Relationship Progression Stages

Each stage has:

- A minimum relationship stat requirement
- A narrative beat (story revelation)
- Optional stat or time-gated choices
- Reward (e.g. item, new dialogue, event)

---

### 🧩 Stage 1 – Acquaintance

- **Stat Requirement:** None (initial contact)
- **Trigger:** First meaningful interaction (e.g. shared class or club)
- **Scene Summary:** Casual dialogue, impression of their outer persona
- **Player Choice Impact:** Sets tone (respect, teasing, admiration)
- **Reward:** Name added to contacts, NPC visible in dorm lounge after school

---

### 🧩 Stage 2 – Budding Friendship

- **Stat Requirement:** Relationship ≥ 10
- **Trigger:** Invite to spend lunch or help with small task
- **Scene Summary:** Learns about hobbies or quirks
- **Player Choice:** Empathy or curiosity unlocks deeper conversation
- **Reward:** Minor stat bonus (e.g. +1 Mood after talking with them)

---

### 🧩 Stage 3 – Vulnerability

- **Stat Requirement:** Relationship ≥ 25, Mood ≥ 30
- **Trigger:** Catch them alone after a bad day or during an event
- **Scene Summary:** First cracks in their persona, hint at hidden trait
- **Player Choice:** Comfort or joke to deflect
- **Reward:** Special location or shared hangout unlocked

---

### 🧩 Stage 4 – Trust

- **Stat Requirement:** Relationship ≥ 50, multiple encounters
- **Trigger:** NPC initiates a personal conversation or invites player out
- **Scene Summary:** Backstory begins unfolding
- **Player Choice:** Ask hard questions or listen quietly
- **Reward:** Secret trait is partially revealed, unlocks custom gift option

---

### 🧩 Stage 5 – Emotional Climax

- **Stat Requirement:** Relationship ≥ 75, triggered by narrative event (e.g. club failure, personal loss, school trip)
- **Scene Summary:** NPC confronts their inner struggle
- **Player Choice:** Offer support or challenge them
- **Reward:** Secret fully revealed, unique cutscene unlocked

---

### 🧩 Stage 6 – Relationship Resolution

- **Stat Requirement:** Relationship ≥ 90
- **Trigger:** Player chooses to define relationship (close friend, romance, or distant respect)
- **Scene Summary:** End-of-year moment, summary of their growth
- **Reward:** Final badge/memory item, visible in ending slideshow

---

## 🔁 Optional Recurring Events

- **Weekly check-ins:** If in same club or seat group
- **Holiday event tie-in:** e.g. Christmas, Valentine’s Day, Spring Trip
- **Random encounters:** In dorms, cafeteria, library
