# 🗺️ Town Locations

Below is a list of 20 explorable places outside the high school campus that players can visit for leisure, shopping,
events, or relationship development.

---

## 1. 🎧 Pixel Pulse Arcade

**Type:** Activity / Hangout  
**Features:** Rhythm games, fighting cabinets, co-op shooters  
**Effects:** Boosts Mood, unlocks unique scenes with Gamer or Flirt  
**Items:** Rare gacha figurines (gift items)

---

## 2. 📚 Evergreen Public Library

**Type:** Quiet Study / Event Trigger  
**Features:** Study cubicles, computer access, poetry open mic  
**Effects:** +Knowledge, +Mood if introverted  
**NPC Tie-ins:** Quiet Genius, Loner, Overachiever

---

## 3. 🍜 Tanaka Noodle House

**Type:** Café / Relationship Hangout

**Effects:** Restores Energy & Mood
